A day in the life.
What our volunteers do.

After sharing with them lunch we spend quality time with the kiddies playing sports, making music, drawing and many more. Our volunteers will be taken care of 24h, from the day of arrival till their departure.

Our students-project.
Teaching in South-Africa.

In South Africa there are dramatic changes and optimism. Allot of our Schools are mostly over-Crowded, understaffed and under-funded, and enthusiasm is evident and Volunteers are needed to cultivate this.

Teaching volunteers.
What they do.

Most Teaching Volunteers get involved in at least 2 of The Main teaching areas, working as a Teacher, Assistant or participating in the literacy or music.
